Welcome to Sanna's "ruska" adventures!


Welcome to explore the magic of Lapland with us. We arrange hiking and mountain biking tours, where you can see the Autumn colors, or as we say in Finland "ruska". If you are very lucky, you can also see the Nothern lights. You can choose 4 different tours. Hiking 2h, hiking 4h, mountain biking 2h and mountain biking 4h. On request we can plan a special tour just for your group. For example for your company's refreshment day. Tours are arranged from August to October.
